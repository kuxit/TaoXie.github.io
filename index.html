About Me

Hello! I am Tao Xie (谢涛), a third-year B.S. student in the Outstanding Engineer Class of Automation at Guangdong University of Technology, advised by Prof. Yiqun Zhang. I conduct research in the SIGMTA group at the OMG Lab.

My research focuses on time series machine learning, encompassing supervised classification, anomaly detection, forecasting, representation learning, as well as unsupervised pattern discovery and clustering. Application domains include medical sepsis classification and industrial detection. Related works have been published in international conferences such as KDD, BIBM, and PRICAI.
